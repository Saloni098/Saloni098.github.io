About me

Hey, I'm Saloni Dwivedi, a product designer, designing for the past 3 years across AI, tax & compliance, fintech and web3.

6 years ago, I got into an engineering college majoring in computer science. I started writing code and making small frontend projects and slowly realized that I really enjoy the solving problems and designing products.

So I decided to pivot completely and started focusing fully on design. I enjoy every bit of being a full time designer and feel very grateful that I took the right call at the right moment.

Life came full circle when AI tools made it easy to code. Now writing code is part of my workflow and I feel complete as a designer.

Apart from design I like sunlight, fresh air, sky, beaches, mountains, cats, trees.
